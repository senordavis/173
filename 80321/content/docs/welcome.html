Hi Everyone, My name is Mike Davis and I am your instructor for ARTS 173, Computer Graphics / Web Design.

ARTS 173 is a course in the effective visual presentation of ideas for the web, from concept and design to structure, preparation, and production. This course focuses on the elements and principles of design as applied to layout, graphics, and animation.

To get started please go to the Content section of the course and go through the Getting Started module and read the Syllabus.

You will find all the assignments and projects are located in a module on its own. This should help you keep track of each assignment that we work on. They will be made available at the start of every week throughout the semester. And each assignment is due at the end of each week.

Your first assignment is to Set up your site and folders. You will have access to is on Tuesday, January 10, 2017. For now please just read the Syllabus.
