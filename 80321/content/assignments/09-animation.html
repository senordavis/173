CSS Animation

With the introduction of CSS 3 we are now capable of animating objects. Support for this is rapidly growing amongst the major browsers. When used properly, animation on the web can add a touch of interest to your pages.

The human brain is hardwired to pay attention to moving objects. Because of this natural reflex to notice movement, adding animation to your website is a powerful way to draw users attention to important areas of your site and add interest to your design.

Objectives

  • Create a fun, purposeful animation using CSS3.
  • If your animation loops, it must cycle seamlessly.

Assignment Specifications

CSS3 animation has many features. While the demo will cover some of the features, it will not cover all of them. Any other techniques you wish to use, you must do the research on your own.

Targeting Webkit browsers (Chrome and Safari), create a CSS Animation that runs for a least 10 seconds. This animation can be a slideshow or an title intro. Anything that adds to the experience of the site.
